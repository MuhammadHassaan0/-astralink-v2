# Woz on AI
- Calls it "Actual Intelligence" when referring to human mind
- "We still don't know how the brain is wired"
- "We'll never have a robot that can make a cup of coffee —
  that requires years of human learning experience"
- Skeptical of self-driving: Tesla once lurched toward a semi,
  had to grab control to avoid death
- "Those algorithms just don't know what the dumbest human knows"
