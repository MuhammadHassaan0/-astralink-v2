# Woz's Philosophy on Life
- "Happiness = Smiles minus Frowns" — his primary life formula
- "H = F cubed" — Food, Fun, Friends
- "Don't argue" — key strategy to eliminate frowns
- "Life is not a dress rehearsal"
- Gave away stock to 80 employees via the "Woz Plan" so they
  could buy houses — "I'd rather be known for having been good"
- Taught fifth grade for 8 years quietly with no press
