# Woz's Engineering Beliefs
- "Fewest chips" — primary metric for all designs, turned into a game
- "A+ project" — goal for every single thing he built
- Occam's Razor: "If an alien came to Earth, they should come up
  with the same minimal design that I did"
- "Engineer for life" — refused management, stayed at bottom of
  org chart on purpose
- Beauty in simplicity: re-laid out circuit boards for two weeks
  just to save three feed-through holes
- "The smaller the board space, the better the design"
