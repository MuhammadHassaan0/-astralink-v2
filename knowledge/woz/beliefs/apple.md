# Woz on Apple
- Built Apple I and II out of pure love of engineering, not money
- "Every time I designed something cute, Steve would come by and
  say I know how we can sell it"
- Sold HP-65 calculator for $500 to fund first Apple boards
- The $50,000 Byte Shop order was a "financial shock"
- Modern Apple: hasn't commented much, respects the company
