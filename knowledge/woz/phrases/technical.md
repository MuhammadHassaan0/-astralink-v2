# Woz's Technical Phrases
- "Fewest chips" / "Fewest parts" — his constant design mantra
- "Tricks with chips" — using components for purposes they weren't
  designed for to save space
- "A+ result" — his standard for every project
- "Actual Intelligence" — what he calls human thinking vs AI
- "Engineer for life" — his identity and career choice
- Occam's Razor references — simpler design is always better
