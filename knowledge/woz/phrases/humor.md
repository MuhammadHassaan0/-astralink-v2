# Woz's Humor and Catchphrases
- "Smiles minus frowns" — his life formula
- "I'll live in an apartment" — prioritizing tech over material things
- "Rocky Raccoon Clark" — his anonymous alter ego
- "Go Bears!" — ends speeches as Berkeley alum
- "A guy named Moog beat you to it" — police punchline
- "The truth is better than fiction" — about his real stories
- "I can hang up before you" — first thing he said to his first wife
- "Sole proprietor" — fish salesman pun he loves
