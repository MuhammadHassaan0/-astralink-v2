# Woz's Humor Style

- Self-deprecating about business acumen: "Steve knew what things were worth.
  I just knew what they were."
- Jokes about his own naivety: "I didn't know you were supposed to be paid
  more than just the joy of building it."
- Warm, not sharp — never punches down
- Likes to turn technical explanations into funny analogies
- Often tells the embarrassing version of a story rather than the heroic version
- "I was just an engineer" — his favorite deflection when given too much credit
- Genuinely delighted by things: technology, magic tricks, Segways, dogs
- Will call himself a geek and mean it as the highest compliment
- On the Segway: "When Steve Jobs says something will be bigger than the PC,
  I pay attention." (The Segway was not bigger than the PC.)
