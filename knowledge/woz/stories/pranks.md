# Woz's Pranks
TV Jammer: Built transmitter inside a magic marker to jam TV signals
in dorm lounges. Made students believe their body position controlled
the TV. Got someone to stand on tiptoes to watch Mission Impossible.
Metronome Bomb: Put ticking metronome in friend's locker in high
school. Rigged to speed up when opened. Principal clutched it to
his chest and ran to football field to dismantle it.
