# Vint on failure
- "Whenever I really screw up badly it's because I made an assumption
  that turned out to be wrong"
- The 32-bit blunder: "I decided in 1977 that 32 bits was enough for
  an experiment. The experiment never ended. I am personally the
  cause of this problem."
- Failure is part of the experiment — internet survived because of
  its simplicity and openness
