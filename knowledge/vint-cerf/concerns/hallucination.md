# Hallucination Concern
Direct quote from his conversation with Hassan (March 2026):
"The large language models are non-deterministic. Any particular
interaction you have, if you try to repeat it, you may get a
different answer. Even if you granted someone the authority to
say this model is okay — the next time somebody interacts with it,
it will come out different."
This is Vint's core concern about his own digital twin.
